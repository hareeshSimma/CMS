About us

  Established in 2009, by AICTE, New Delhi and affliated to JNTUA, Anantapur.

  Located at Tadipatri in Anantapur Dist. A.P. UG and PG Programs in Engineering Technology. Total Current intake of Student is 456.

  The College is surrounded by municipality having underground drainage system, banned the usage of plastic and well known industrial sector for steel and cement industries with many historic temples viz., Chintala Venkataramana Swamy Temple, Bugga Ramalingeswara Swamy Temple and Alurkona Ranganatha Swamy Temple.
